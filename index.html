Breaking

News that matter to you,
right in the today tab.

Meet Breaking, a little app that let’s you add
RSS feeds to your ‘Today tab’ in Yosemite.

Add local news, your favorite blogs or anything else that comes with an RSS feed as a ‘Today’ Widget in OS X Yosemite. The latest stories will always be right there when you have time for them, a simple gesture away. Breaking is an easy, uncluttered way of staying on top of what’s new in your world.

A Great Way to Catch Up On Headlines

Breaking marries the accessibility of the Today view with the power of RSS feeds. A News overview, only a gesture away.

Multiple Feeds

Follow multiple news outlets? (you should). Breaking merges unlimited feeds and displays the 5 latest stories.

We’ll find the feed for you

Just type in the url and we’ll search the site and find the feed for you. It’s really easy.

Build for Yosemite

Breaking was build specifically for digesting news on OS X Yosemite.
